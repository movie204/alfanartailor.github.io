✦ About Alfanar

Where Dubai's elegance
meets master craft

Alfanar Tailor & Embroidery is rooted in Dubai's rich culture of quality and refinement. We serve the UAE's diverse community — from Emirati nationals seeking the perfect Kandura, to business professionals needing sharp bespoke suits, to brides wanting their dream gown.

Every piece leaves our workshop with the same promise: impeccable fit, premium fabric, and artisan-level finishing. That is the Alfanar standard.
